Department of Information Technology

The Department of Information Technology, established in 1999, with an intake of 40 at undergraduate level. Presently the department has intake of 120 in undergraduate program B.Tech. (IT). Since start of the course it has undergone major developments. The department has qualified, experienced faculty in adequate number in cadre ratio. The department has laboratories like Networking, Project, Programming and RDBMS with modern facilities, departmental library and a computer center.The department encourages its faculty to present papers in National Journal, Referred International Journals, Conferences Symposium and Workshops/Staff Development Program like TEQIP-III, ISTE and STP. It has good placement records in prominent IT companies Infosys, Wipro, Cognizent, Amazon etc.The department publishes a National Journal entitled ‘National Journal of Engineering Science and Management’. Research assistance for paper presentation and travel grants for India and abroad are provided by Barkhatullaha University and government agencies like MPCST, AICTE, DST etc.The department has completed AICTE-MODROBS project of Rs. 5 Lacs for the establishment of Open Source (Linux/Unix) Laboratory under the guidance of Dr. Manish Shrivastava as the Principal Investigator.

Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the college was established in year 1994 with intake of 60 students. Two postgraduate courses in Computer Science and Engineering and Software Engineering were started in the year 2004 and 2007 respectively.

Currently the department has an intake of 180 at undergraduate level and 24 at postgraduate level (M.Tech, CSE). Since its inception the department has always been recognized for excellence in teaching and outstanding students’ performance at global level and for the excellent placement record in various international/national companies. All over the world, the alumni of the department occupy top positions in IT industries like Google, Sun Microsystem, Yahoo, Cisco, TCS, Cognizant, Infosys, Wipro and many more.

Department provides an ambient teaching learning environment for its students through qualified, experienced faculty and smart classroom facility. The course structure is as per Barkhatullaha University, Bhopal and includes courses on nascent topics to equip our students with the latest developments in Computer Science Engineering to bridge the gap with the industry.

Research guidance to M. Tech. students is provided by Head of Department and the Head of Computer Center, along with the various other experienced faculty members in the areas like Networks Security, Cloud Computing, Data Mining and Knowledge Management, Soft Computing, Image Processing, Artificial Intelligence, Ad-hoc Networks, Parallel Processing, Software Engineering etc.. The department also organizes National/International Conference, Workshops, Seminar, Expert Lectures in above mentioned areas.

The Department has 10 nos. of state-of-the-art computer laboratories with latest computers and software supported by High Speed Ethernet and Wireless networks. Presently the department has leased line Internet connectivity on OFC(Primary) and RF(Backup). Various student Chapters/Organizations like CSI Chapter, IETE Chapter, Open SUN Solaris Community are active throughout the year.

Research assistance for paper presentation and travel grants for India and abroad are provided by LNCT and government agencies like MPCST, AICTE, DST etc.
