Barkatullah University is a state government university in Bhopal, India. Originally known as the University of Bhopal, and informally as Bhopal University, it was renamed in 1988 after the freedom fighter Professor Maulavi Barkatullah, who was born in the area. Barkatullah University has been accredited as an A category university by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).
